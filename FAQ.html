FAQ

FAQ.


General information


This section has been prepared by the technical support specialists with the purpose of helping the investors to get the answers to the most frequent questions

I am a beginner investor. How do I start my activity?

Before you start investing, you should go through the registration procedure. It is not difficult to do and it should take just a few minutes of your time. Before the beginning of registration, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the rules of cooperation between the Coin-Invest company and the investor.

How do I successfully register?

To start the registration, click on the “Registration” button on the website. Then, fill out all the required fields as directed.

It is forbidden to use fake information because it might lead to various misunderstandings in the future.

Can I create several accounts from one IP address?

No, you cannot. Each user is allowed to register only one account. In case of violation of this paragraph of the rules, all of the profiles will be frozen without the return of the funds that are on the balance.

What should I do to get the status of the company’s investor? What are the ways of getting the profit?

After the completion of the registration on the website, the user must make a deposit. 24 hours after the deposit, the first profit will be placed on the investor’s account. This sum will be available for withdrawal from the system immediately.

What are the working hours of Coin-Global company?

The company works in a 24/7 regime, without breaks or days off.


Information about the finances


How can I make a deposit into my personal account?

You can make a deposit into your personal account with the help of one of the payment systems (we will get familiarized with them on the main page of the company’s web resource)

What is the minimum and maximum amount of the deposit?

The investor has a right to make a deposit into his account in the amount from $20 to 1BTC.

At what time of the day can I withdraw the profit?

You can withdraw the profit regardless of the time of day, right after the funds were placed onto your account’s balance.

Can I make a deposit and withdrawal with the help of various payment systems?

Deposits and withdrawals from one account are possible only by using one payment system.

How long does it take to process the request for profit withdrawal?

withdrawal is usually paid instantly.

Is there a fee for making a deposit or withdrawal of funds?

The Coin-Invest company doesn’t take any additional fees for deposits/withdrawals of funds. There may be a commission of the payment system through which the movement of your funds is implemented.


A partnership program (Affiliate)


Do the company's rules provide for an affiliate program?

Yes, we have a partnership program. You can get familiarized with its terms in the appropriate section of our website.

How do I become a participant in the partnership program?

Everyone who completes the registration procedure can become a participant in the partnership program.

Do I need to make a deposit in order to become a participant in the partnership program?

No. Earnings on attracting new investors to the company do not imply mandatory investing. You can cooperate with the company without making a deposit.


Guarantees and safety


What guarantees of safety are offered in the cooperation?

Complete protection of personal information about the user suggests the use of SSL certificates and SSL encryptions by the company, which provides the reliability of the server’s work and protection of data.

We also use the special software developed for the protection of each investor’s personal data from DDOS attacks and illegal access to them.

How can I be sure of Coin-Global company’s honesty when it comes to working with investors?

The reputation of the company and each investor is of great value to us. Our responsibility is to offer the uninterrupted operation of site services work, timely accrual of profit and its guaranteed payments.
